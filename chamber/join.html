Designed for non-profit organizations committed to community service.

  • Basic Directory Listing
  • Access to General Workshops & Webinars
  • Community Engagement Opportunities
  • Networking Events (Standard Access)
  • Chamber Newsletter Subscription

Bronze Membership Benefits

Ideal for new businesses looking to establish a local presence.

  • All NP Membership Benefits
  • Enhanced Directory Listing
  • Member-Exclusive Discounts on Chamber Events
  • Basic Business Resources & Templates
  • Opportunity to Host Small Networking Events

Silver Membership Benefits

Perfect for growing businesses seeking increased visibility and support.

  • All Bronze Membership Benefits
  • Priority Listing in Directory
  • Access to Advanced Training & Workshops
  • Social Media Mentions & Promotion (Quarterly)
  • Invitations to Exclusive Luncheons & Receptions
  • Discounted Advertising Rates in Chamber Publications

Gold Membership Benefits

Our premium tier for established businesses aiming for maximum impact and leadership.

  • All Silver Membership Benefits
  • Featured Spotlight Position on Homepage (Monthly Rotation)
  • Priority for Sponsorship Opportunities at Major Events
  • Dedicated Business Consultation (Annual)
  • Premium Advertising Placement in Chamber Newsletter & Website
  • Exclusive Invitation to Board Meetings & Leadership Roundtables
  • Personalized Business Referrals
