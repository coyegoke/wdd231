Join Our Chamber

Becoming a member of the Timcornels Chamber of Commerce connects you with a network of local businesses and provides valuable resources to help your business thrive. Choose the membership level that best suits your needs:

Non-Profit Membership

Free for eligible non-profits

  • Basic Directory Listing
  • Access to general workshops
  • Community engagement opportunities

Silver Membership

$50/month

  • Enhanced Directory Listing
  • Access to all workshops and trainings
  • Social media mentions
  • Invitations to exclusive luncheons
  • Networking event discounts

Gold Membership

$150/month

  • Premium Directory Listing (featured)
  • All Silver benefits included
  • Monthly business spotlight in newsletter
  • Priority for sponsorship opportunities
  • Dedicated advertising space on website
  • One-on-one business consultation
