Discover Ife

Our History

Ife boasts a rich history, dating back centuries as a significant cultural and spiritual center. Its vibrant past has shaped its present, creating a community deeply rooted in tradition while embracing progress. Discover the tales of ancient kingdoms and the evolution of our beloved city.

Demographics

The city of Ife is a melting pot of cultures and ages, with a diverse population contributing to its dynamic atmosphere. Our community is characterized by a strong workforce, a growing number of young families, and a welcoming environment for newcomers. Detailed demographic data is available upon request.

Current Events

Ife is always buzzing with activity! From local festivals and art exhibitions to business workshops and community gatherings, there's always something happening. Check our events calendar regularly for the latest updates and opportunities to engage with our vibrant community.

  • Ife Annual Business Summit - August 15-17, 2025
  • Local Artisans Market - Every Saturday at Central Square
  • Community Clean-Up Day - September 10, 2025
